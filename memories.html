22 of My Favorite Memories

10. When you helped me dress better and dragged me to the ukay-ukay to shop. I never thought I’d be thankful for secondhand clothes, but you made it fun.

11. You put so much effort into making my birthday special, and even got mad at me for not getting excited enough. Haha, I loved how you cared so much.

12.That night we had ice cream because I was so drunk and needed to sober up. You gave me the best “good-natured” nagging, and honestly, you kept me in check.

19. Our monthsary at McDo. You nearly finished the entire 1.5L of Coke, and I couldn’t stop laughing. You made the day extra special in the funniest way.

21. That time we went on the Viking ride — I was absolutely terrified, and I almost fainted from fear. I was so scared of the ride that I completely forgot you were by my side, but somehow, you kept me calm.

22. And the Ferris wheel — I thought I was going to pass out from the height, but I completely forgot I wasn’t alone because I was so freaked out. But you were right there, holding my hand, making everything feel a little less scary.

There are so many more... I'll just wait until you turn 1,000 years old so I can list them all!
